# Sales & Profitability Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

## Overview
This project delivers an interactive **Sales & Profitability Analytics dashboard built in Excel**, designed to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights on **sales performance, profit margins, product contribution, and regional trends**.

The dashboard enables business users to evaluate **revenue growth, cost impact, and overall profitability** through structured visuals, pivot analysis, and dynamic filtering.

---

## Objectives
- Convert raw sales transactions into **actionable business insights**
- Track **revenue and profit performance** across time, products, and regions
- Identify **top-performing and loss-making segments**
- Support **data-driven pricing and strategic decisions**

---

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Sales Overview
- KPIs: **Total Sales, Total Profit, Order Volume, Profit Margin %**
- **Monthly and yearly sales trend analysis**
- Snapshot of overall business performance with Quaterly sales analysis

### 2. Product, Regional & Profitability Analysis
- Identification of **top revenue-generating, high-profit, and loss-making products**
- Revenue and profitability comparison by **region, market, and customer segment**
- Detection of **high-growth vs low-growth geographic areas** and their contribution to total sales
- Analysis of **profit margin distribution** and the impact of **pricing range, and quantity** on overall profitability
- Clear visibility into **key profit drivers and weak business segments** to support strategic decision-making

---

## Interactivity
- Dynamic **slicers and filters** for:
  - Year / Month  
  - Region / Segment  
  - Product Category  
- Drill-down capability for **detailed performance exploration**
- Trend comparison across **time periods**

---

## Data Sources

### Data Tables Used
The dashboard is created using structured Excel datasets, including:

- **Sales Transactions** – Order ID, date, product, quantity, sales, profit, discount, segment  
- **Product Details** – Category, sub-category, and product information  
- **Regional Mapping** – Region, state, or market classification  
- **Time Dimension** – Month and year fields for trend analysis  

---

## Data Cleaning & Transformation
- **Power Query** used for:
  - Removing nulls and duplicates  
  - Standardizing date and text formats  
  - Creating calculated columns (Year, Month, Profit Margin %)  
  - Structuring clean analytical tables for reporting  

---

## Tools & Techniques
- **Microsoft Excel** – Dashboard design and visualization  
- **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- **Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts** – Aggregation and trend analysis  
- **Excel Formulas / Calculated Fields** – KPI and profitability metrics  
- **Slicers & Filters** – Interactive exploration
